Introduction

Summary

Opened in 2023, the school aims to develop engineers and scientists in the fields of nanotechnology and nanoscience. Located in the Abdelhafid Ihddaden Pole and inaugurated by the president in May 2024, it offers a variety of accommodations for students, marking a significant step toward advancing our nation's development

Creation

This school was created one year ahead of schedule. It was a hard project for the manager, Azzaz Mohamed, And the students.Throughout this year, we had to use the laboratory of USTHB due to our unfinished laboratory. However, insha'Allah, we will start working in our own lab starting from this September.

Genesis Class

This is the name we—the students—chose to be our class name, and it means 'the origin or coming into being of something.' We chose this name to represent what we went through this year. We are grateful for both the good and the bad. We also hope that the situation of our school continues to improve to meet international standards.

Scientific Clubs

The school administration was unable to establish legal scientific clubs this year due to delays in processing the school's paperwork. This situation has been a disappointment for many of us who were exited to participate in the scientific events. However, the manager has assured us that starting from this September, the administration will take the necessary steps to organize the official creation of the scientific clubs, allowing students to fully engage in these valuable activities.

Exelence

Excellent student privilleges of the science and technology hub

The campus is conveniently located just 2 minutes away from the school, offering easy access for students. Each room is individual, providing students with privacy and a comfortable living environment. The campus staff are known for their professionalism and helpfulness, ensuring a supportive atmosphere for students. Additionally, the food served on campus is consistently rated as satisfying and nutritious, catering to diverse tastes and dietary preferences.

The students receive a stipend of 10,000 Algerian dinars per month, which is considered generous and supportive. This financial assistance helps students manage their expenses effectively while focusing on their studies, ensuring they can fully engage in their academic pursuits without financial stress.

In the Abdelhafid Ihddaden Pole, located in Sidi Abdallah, Algiers, we proudly offer five distinguished schools: the Higher School of Artificial Intelligence, Mathematics, Nanoscience and Nanotechnology, Autonomous Systems, and Cybersecurity. This collection of institutions represents a strong commitment to excellence in advanced technology and sciences, catering to the evolving demands of modern education and industry.

The school provides a comprehensive academic program, starting with a solid foundation in the form of a 2-year preparatory course in Science and Technology, followed by 3 years of specialized studies. Currently, students can choose from specialties such as Nano Fabrication and Nano Materials. This structured curriculum ensures students receive thorough preparation and expertise in their chosen fields, equipping them with the knowledge and skills necessary for successful careers in advanced technology and sciences.
